About Me

Hi, I'm Rosalie. I am a journalism and computer science student based in Chicago. I am currently a sophomore at Northwestern University. I am exploring multimedia, news writing and feature writing, as well as the intersection of journalism and technology. I have written, edited and produced multimedia content for various publications, and in my free time, I experiment with photography, videography and coding. I coded this website out of scratch, so feel free to send me any comments or critiques. I believe every person has a story, and I am especially interested in reporting on diversity, race, education, immigration, and Asian and Latino issues. Other than that, I love reading, card games, running, my cats and updating my endless Spotify playlists. You can view my resume here.
